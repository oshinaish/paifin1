Formula to Determine Car Loan EMI

Our calculator uses the industry-standard formula to calculate your Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for your car loan:

EMI =
P × R × (1 + R)N
(1 + R)N - 1

Where P is the Principal Loan Amount, R is the monthly interest rate, and N is the number of months. Your investment growth is calculated using the standard future value of a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) formula.

FAQs for Car Loan Calculator

What is a car loan EMI calculator?

A car loan EMI calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate the monthly amount you need to pay towards your car loan. The PaiFinance calculator goes a step further by also showing you how you can invest alongside your EMI payments to build wealth.

How is car loan EMI calculated?

Car loan EMI is calculated based on three main factors: the principal loan amount, the interest rate, and the loan tenure. Our calculator uses the standard EMI formula to give you an accurate monthly payment amount.
